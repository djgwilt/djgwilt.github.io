Instructions

Player 1

Player 1 starts in the top left corner. Use arrow keys to move.
Don't crash into walls.
Monsters (and Player 2 if there is one) will kill you.
Coloured squares give you immunity to the monster of the colour.
Immunities are not permanent.
Complete the game in less moves to get a higher score.
You need to have all 3 coins and get to the finish flag to win.

Good luck.
Don't die.

Player 2 (optional)

Player 2 starts in the middle of the top row. Use WASD to move.
Don't crash into walls.
Monsters do not kill you.
Your job is to catch Player 1 to kill them and stop them from finishing.
